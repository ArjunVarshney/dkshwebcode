Efficiently Reduce Work Complexity to Drive Global Market Sales

HomeCase Studies
  • Market: Global (Asia + Europe, covered more than 10 markets +++)

Challenge

They manage three applications for customer sales, customer support, and supplier management. The primary challenge lies in the disconnected nature of these applications from vital data sources, necessary for enhancing customer relationship management and driving increased business revenue. Given the extensive market coverage, they require a simplified workflow, real-time functionality, and the eliminate of separate workflows across different applications to efficiently capture data.

Solution

We offer them access to 3 APIs, integrating siloed of customer and sales data to expedite time-to-market:

  • Customer Create Sales Order API
  • Trace Order Get Status API
  • Product Get Product Stock Detail API

Result

  • Reduce operations complexity by centralizing API calls for all applications.
  • Consolidated data management leads to easier access to data sources.
  • Standardize and govern workflows for efficient data exchange.
